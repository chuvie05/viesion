I'm Vie, and this is Viesion.

I am a Vietnamese freelance illustrator and UI/UX designer based in Melbourne. With both skills combined, I create work that is not just visually distinctive and eye-catching, but meaningful — visuals that resonate and interfaces that feel intentional.

M Y   W O R K S

Vietera

A website with a game mechanic designed to make learning Vietnamese culture fun and interactive. Unpack card packs to discover unique cultural icons — from bánh mì to áo dài — each with its own illustrated card and cultural introduction.

Illustration Coding Graphic Design

[Re-design] Unilodge Mobile App

As both a resident and former staff at Unilodge, I experienced firsthand how difficult the existing app was to navigate. I redesigned it — keeping the existing aesthetic while significantly improving the user experience, tested with both residents and non-residents.

UI/UX

Tangled

A hair-care companion app for those who love experimenting with new hairstyles but struggle with maintaining healthy hair. Tangled personalises your hair-care journey with tailored routines and product recommendations based on your hair type and goals.

UI/UX Graphic Design

Velicious

A handy app for the vegan community — combining a social forum, recipe sharing, nutrition planning, restaurant finder, and product label scanner into one platform. Designed to help people stay vegan in a logical, healthy, and connected way.

UI/UX

A short frame-by-frame 2D animation raising awareness about animal abuse in circuses — told through a reversed perspective where humans become the victims. Entirely hand-drawn in Procreate, with sound design in Premiere Pro.
